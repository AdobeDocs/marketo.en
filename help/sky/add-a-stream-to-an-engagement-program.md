# Add a Stream to an Engagement Program

Engagement programs can hold more than one stream.

1. Select your engagement program.

   ![Image One](/help/sky/assets/engagement-programs/add-a-stream-to-an-engagement-program/add-a-stream-to-an-engagement-program-1.png)

1. Click the **[!UICONTROL Streams]** tab.

   ![Image Two](/help/sky/assets/engagement-programs/add-a-stream-to-an-engagement-program/add-a-stream-to-an-engagement-program-2.png)

1. Click **[!UICONTROL Add Stream]**.

   ![Image Three](/help/sky/assets/engagement-programs/add-a-stream-to-an-engagement-program/add-a-stream-to-an-engagement-program-3.png)

>[!NOTE]
>
>You can add up to 25 streams per engagement program.
