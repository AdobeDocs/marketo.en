# Create an Email Program in Program Schedule View
<br>&nbsp;

You can create new email programs directly in the program schedule view.

1. Select your program in the tree, then go to the **Schedule** tab under **Overview**.

   ![Image One](/help/sky/assets/program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view-1.png)

1. Hover over the day you want to add an entry to, then click **Add**.

   ![Image Two](/help/sky/assets/program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view-2.png)

   >[!NOTE]
   >
   >You can also follow similar steps to create an email program from the Day, Week, or Agenda views.

1. Enter a name for your new smart campaign, then change **Type** to Email Program. You can also modify the **Start Date**, and uncheck **Open In Agenda View** if you want to stay in the calendar after creating the entry. Click **Save**.

   ![Image Three](/help/sky/assets/program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view-3.png)

1. You can now find your email program in the calendar. Hover over it for details...

   ![Image Four](/help/sky/assets/program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view-4.png)

...or click to open the editor window.

   ![Image Five](/help/sky/assets/program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view/create-an-email-program-in-program-schedule-view-5.png)

>[!NOTE]
>
>You can create email programs within the program schedule view in Marketo Sky, but the actual program setup will be in
Marketo Classic.
