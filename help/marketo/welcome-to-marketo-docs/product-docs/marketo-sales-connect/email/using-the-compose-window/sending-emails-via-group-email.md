# Sending Emails via Group Email {#sending-emails-via-group-email}

Sending Emails via Group Email - Marketo Docs - Product Documentation

Here's how to send/edit emails using the Group Email option.

### What's in this article? {#what-s-in-this-article}

[Sending a Group Email](#sendingemailsviagroupemail-sendingagroupemail)  
[Editing a Group Email](#sendingemailsviagroupemail-editingagroupemail)

#### Sending a Group Email {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-sendingagroupemail}

##### 1. Click the People tab. {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-clickthepeopletab.}

![](assets/one-3.png)

##### 2. Select the group you'd like to email. {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-selectthegroupyou'dliketoemail.}

![](assets/two-3.png)

##### 3. Click the Group Actions button and select Email Group. {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-clickthegroupactionsbuttonandselectemailgroup.}

![](assets/three-3.png)

##### 4. Fill out your email (or select a template) and send (or schedule) it. {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-filloutyouremail(orselectatemplate)andsend(orschedule)it.}

![](assets/four-3.png)

#### Editing a Group Email {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-editingagroupemail}

##### 1. Create a group email using [Steps 1-3 above](https://docs.marketo.com/display/DOCS/Sending+Emails+via+Group+Email#SendingEmailsviaGroupEmail-SendingaGroupEmail). {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-createagroupemailusingsteps1-3above.}

##### 2. Choose a template, or fill out your email. {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-chooseatemplate-orfilloutyouremail.}

![](assets/edit-two.png)

##### 3. With the email done, you can now preview each email in the list to see if the dynamic fields are populating correctly. {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-withtheemaildone-youcannowprevieweachemailinthelisttoseeifthedynamicfieldsarepopulatingcorrectly.}

![](assets/edit-three.png)

##### 4. Select the desired recipient. {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-selectthedesiredrecipient.}

![](assets/edit-four.png)

##### 5. Click Preview Dynamic Fields, and view the preview on the right. {#sendingemailsviagroupemail-clickpreviewdynamicfields-andviewthepreviewontheright.}

![](assets/edit-five.png)

>[!NOTE]
>
>You can make bulk edits to the email/template when sending a group email out, but you cannot make unique edits for specific recipients in the list.
