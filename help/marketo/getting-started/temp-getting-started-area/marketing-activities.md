# NEW AREA: Marketing Activities Checklist {#new-area-marketing-activities-checklist}

Marketing Activities houses all the assets and content that make up your automated marketing programs. As you set up a new Marketo Engage instance, a clean and clear organization ensures all users can easily find and manage various programs. Track your configuration progress and check off essential steps offline with downloadable checklists -LINK HERE-
