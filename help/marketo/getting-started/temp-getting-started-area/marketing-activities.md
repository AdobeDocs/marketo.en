# NEW AREA: Marketing Activities Checklist {#new-area-marketing-activities-checklist}

Marketing Activities houses all the assets and content that make up your automated marketing programs. As you set up a new instance, a clean and clear organization ensures all users can easily find and manage various programs within your Marketo Engage instance. Track your configuration progress and check off essential steps offline with downloadable checklists -LINK HERE-
