# Log Inbound Mail From Your Leads in Marketo {#log-inbound-mail-from-your-leads-in-marketo}

Log Inbound Mail From Your Leads in Marketo - Marketo Docs - Product Documentation

You can log replies from your leads right in Outlook with the Marketo Email Add-In.

### What's in this article? {#what-s-in-this-article}

[From the Main Outlook Application](#loginboundmailfromyourleadsinmarketo-fromthemainoutlookapplication)  
[From the Email Itself](#loginboundmailfromyourleadsinmarketo-fromtheemailitself)

#### From the Main Outlook Application {#loginboundmailfromyourleadsinmarketo-fromthemainoutlookapplication}

##### Select the email you want to log and click Log with Marketo. {#loginboundmailfromyourleadsinmarketo-selecttheemailyouwanttologandclicklogwithmarketo}

![](assets/image2014-9-23-17-3a12-3a44.png)

>[!TIP]
>
>You can also right-click on a message and click **Log With Marketo**.

You should see a confirmation.

![](assets/image2014-9-23-17-3a13-3a39.png)

#### From the Email Itself {#loginboundmailfromyourleadsinmarketo-fromtheemailitself}

##### If you have opened the email, you can simply click on the Log with Marketo button from there. {#loginboundmailfromyourleadsinmarketo-ifyouhaveopenedtheemail-youcansimplyclickonthelogwithmarketobuttonfromthere}

![](assets/image2014-9-23-17-3a14-3a14.png)

##### You should see the same confirmation as the other method. {#loginboundmailfromyourleadsinmarketo-youshouldseethesameconfirmationastheothermethod}

![](assets/image2014-9-23-17-3a14-3a29.png)

Log your lead's replies to add them to their history in Marketo.
