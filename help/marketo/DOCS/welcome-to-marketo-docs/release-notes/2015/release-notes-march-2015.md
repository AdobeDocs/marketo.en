#### Calendar HD {#releasenotes-march2015-calendarhd}

[Display your team’s marketing actives](../../../welcome-to-marketo-docs/product-docs/core-marketo-concepts/marketing-calendar/calendar-hd.md) with the calendar’s new presentation mode. These are great for TVs or giant monitors around the office! Set and displays goals based on a smart list or custom metrics.

>[!NOTE]
>
>This feature is not available for Spark and Standard editions.

![](assets/image2015-3-23-11-3a39-3a15.png)  

#### Google Adwords Integration  {#releasenotes-march2015-googleadwordsintegration}

Link your [Google AdWords account to Marketo](../../../welcome-to-marketo-docs/product-docs/administration/additional-integrations/add-google-adwords-as-a-launchpoint-service.md) to automatically upload offline conversion data from Marketo to Google AdWords. Then, from the AdWords UI, you will be able to easily see which clicks resulted in qualified leads, opportunities, and new customers (or whatever revenue stages you want to track).

![](assets/image2015-3-23-11-3a50-3a55.png)

#### Revenue Explorer Redesign {#releasenotes-march2015-revenueexplorerredesign}

[Revenue Explorer](../../../welcome-to-marketo-docs/product-docs/reporting/revenue-cycle-analytics/revenue-explorer.md) has a brand new look and feel, as well as the new Sunburst chart type! We'll be rolling this out over the first two weeks of April.

#### New Asset REST APIs [](http://developers.marketo.com/) {#releasenotes-march2015-newassetrestapis}

We now have support for creating and editing emails, templates, my tokens, files, and snippets [via the API](http://developers.marketo.com/documentation/asset-api)!

#### Microsoft Dynamics 2015 On Premise {#releasenotes-march2015-microsoftdynamics2015onpremise}
