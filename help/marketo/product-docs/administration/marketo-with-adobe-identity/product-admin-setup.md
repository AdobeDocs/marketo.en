# Product Admin Setup {#product-admin-setup}

1. After you have been invited by a System Admin, you will receive a welcome email. In that email, click **[!UICONTROL Get Started]**.

   ![](assets/admin-setup-7.png)

1. If you have previously accessed an application with an Adobe ID, you will be taken straight to the Adobe Admin Console. If not, [set up your Adobe ID](https://helpx.adobe.com/manage-account/using/create-update-adobe-id.html){target="_blank"}.

   ![](assets/admin-setup-8.png)

Product Admins are primarily responsible for adding users. [Learn how to do that here](/help/marketo/product-docs/administration/users-and-roles/add-or-remove-a-user.md#add-a-user){target="_blank"}.
