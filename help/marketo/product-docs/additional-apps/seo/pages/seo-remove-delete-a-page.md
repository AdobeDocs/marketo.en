# SEO - Remove/Delete a Page {#seo-remove-delete-a-page}

Learn how to delete a page.

>[!IMPORTANT]
>
>On March 31, 2026, Marketo Engage will deprecate the Search Engine Optimization feature. Please export any relevant data on or before March 30. [Learn more](https://nation.marketo.com/t5/product-blogs/marketo-engage-seo-feature-deprecation/ba-p/359060){target="_blank"}.

1. Go to the **[!UICONTROL Pages]** section.

   ![](assets/image2014-9-18-13-3a58-3a33.png)

1. On the [!UICONTROL Pages] tab, hover over the page you'd like to remove, click **[!UICONTROL Delete]**, then click **[!UICONTROL Remove]**.

   ![](assets/image2014-9-18-13-3a58-3a39.png)

This page is now permanently removed from your list.
