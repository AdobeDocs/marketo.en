# CX Enterprise Coworker Overview {#overview}

CX Enterprise Coworker is an AI-native marketing application that takes you from a single prompt to a review-ready campaign in minutes.

## How to access

>[!AVAILABILITY]
>
>CX Enterprise Coworker is available as a free trial through September 30, 2026.

1. Go to coworker-essentials.experience.adobe.com.

1. Sign in or create an account using your business email.

   Existing Adobe customers are auto-approved and routed straight to the trial environment to accept the terms.

   New users are prompted to create an account. The CX Enterprise Coworker team reviews requests and approves business emails. You will receive an email when your request is approved.

1. Accept the trial terms.

1. Set up your brand under **Your stuff** > **Brands**. CX Enterprise Coworker uses this as the default for every campaign you generate.

1. (Optional) Upload an HTML email template under **Email templates** for CX Enterprise Coworker to apply your design when generating content.

## What you can do with CX Enterprise Coworker

CX Enterprise Coworker brings five workflows that usually span five different tools into one conversational experience:

- **Turn a brief into a campaign plan**. Drop in a prompt or paste a brief, and CX Enterprise Coworker returns a structured, end-to-end plan.
- **Build and refine audiences**. Upload a CSV or connect your data, then refine the segment in conversation.
- **Generate on-brand content**. CX Enterprise Coworker writes emails that match your brand voice, references, and templates.
- **Design multi-step journeys**. CX Enterprise Coworker assembles nurture sequences, not just one-off sends.
- **Send proof emails**. Preview every message in your own inbox before launch.
- **Launch and analyze**. _(Coming soon)_ Send campaigns and get next-best-step insights without leaving CX Enterprise Coworker.

## How the AI works

CX Enterprise Coworker follows a simple, multi-turn conversational loop:

**Input**. You provide a campaign goal, your audience (uploaded or connected), and optional brand context.

**Processing**. The CX Enterprise Coworker agentic AI generates a campaign plan, builds a journey, and drafts personalized content.

**Output**. A draft campaign appears on your campaign board, ready for review, iteration, and eventual launch.

You can keep talking to CX Enterprise Coworker at any point (for example, "make the second email more urgent," "shorten the subject lines," or "swap the audience to lapsed customers from Q3") and it will update the artifacts in place.

>[!CAUTION]
>
>Always check outputs before sending.

## Tips for getting started

A few things early users have found make a real difference:

- **Start with a specific, real use case**. "Win-back series for customers who have not ordered in 90 days" beats "make me a campaign." See _Use cases_ for specific examples.
- **Clean your audience data**. CX Enterprise Coworker can only personalize as well as your CSV allows. Exclude any contacts you should not be emailing before you upload.
- **Invest in your brand setup**. CX Enterprise Coworker extracts brand details when you sign up. A quick review of **Your stuff** > **Brands** benefits every campaign you generate.
- **Send a proof email**. Before you launch, send a proof and review it in your own inbox.
- **Iterate, do not restart**. Treat CX Enterprise Coworker's first output as a starting point. The fastest path to great is a few rounds of refinement, not a fresh prompt.
- **Export when you need a paper trail**. Export individual emails as HTML or the full campaign as a Word doc or PDF for team review.

## What is available in the trial

CX Enterprise Coworker is a product in active development. Here is what to know going in:

- **Acceptance required**: You will need to review and accept the trial terms before accessing the product.

- **Compliance**: You are responsible for following email regulations (unsubscribe links, privacy policies, etc.).

New capabilities will ship during the trial. Your feedback helps shape what comes next. Submit feedback via the in-product feedback link.
