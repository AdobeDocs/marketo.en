# Halo Overview {#halo-overview}

Halo is an AI-native marketing application that takes you from a single prompt to a review-ready campaign in minutes. Built for lean lifecycle and growth teams in B2B and B2C, Halo combines campaign planning, audience building, content generation, and journey design into one conversational workspace — so you can spend less time switching tools and more time on the work that matters.

What is Halo?

Most marketers stitch together five or more tools to ship a single campaign — planning, segmentation, content, configuration, analysis. That work often stretches across days or weeks.

Halo collapses that workflow into a single, conversational interface. You start with a prompt or a brief, and Halo's agentic AI generates a structured campaign plan, builds your audience, drafts on-brand content, and assembles a multi-step journey — all on one campaign board you can refine in plain language.

You stay in control. Halo generates; you review, refine, and decide what goes live.

Who Halo is for

Halo is built for lifecycle and growth marketers at lean teams who want to launch campaigns faster without trading away quality or control.

B2C marketers running re-engagement, onboarding, newsletters, and seasonal pushes.

B2B marketers running webinar invites, account onboarding, product education, and nurture sequences.

Whether you're a one-person team or part of a small marketing org, Halo helps you do the work of many — without the tool sprawl.

What you can do with Halo

Halo brings five workflows that usually live in five different tools into one conversational experience:

Turn a brief into a campaign plan. Drop in a prompt or paste a brief, and Halo returns a structured, end-to-end plan.

Build and refine audiences. Upload a CSV or connect your data, then refine the segment in conversation.

Generate on-brand content. Halo writes emails that match your brand voice, references, and templates.

Design multi-step journeys. Halo assembles nurture sequences — not just one-off sends.

Send proof emails. Preview every message in your own inbox before launch.

Launch and analyze. (Coming soon) Send campaigns and get next-best-step insights without leaving Halo.

How the AI works

Halo follows a simple, multi-turn conversational loop:

Input. You provide a campaign goal, your audience (uploaded or connected), and optional brand context.

Processing. Halo's agentic AI generates a campaign plan, builds a journey, and drafts personalized content.

Output. A draft campaign appears on your campaign board — ready for review, iteration, and eventual launch.

You can keep talking to Halo at any point — "make the second email more urgent," "shorten the subject lines," "swap the audience to lapsed customers from Q3" — and it will update the artifacts in place.

You're always in the driver's seat. Halo generates; you review, refine, and approve. Always check outputs before sending.

Tips for getting started

A few things early users have found make a real difference:

Start with a specific, real use case. "Win-back series for customers who haven't ordered in 90 days" beats "make me a campaign." Browse Use cases from our collection for inspiration.

Clean your audience data. Halo can only personalize as well as your CSV allows. Exclude any contacts you shouldn't be emailing before you upload.

Invest in your brand setup. Halo extracts brand details when you sign up — but a quick review of Your stuff > Brands pays off across every campaign you generate.

Send a proof email. Before you launch, send a proof and review in your own inbox.

Iterate, don't restart. Treat Halo's first output as a starting point. The fastest path to great is a few rounds of refinement, not a fresh prompt.

Export when you need a paper trail. Export individual emails as HTML or the full campaign as a Word doc or PDF for team review.
