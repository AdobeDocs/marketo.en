# Single Flow Actions from Person Detail Page {#single-flow-actions-from-person-detail-page}

In addition to running single flow actions from within a Smart List, you can also run them directly on a Person Detail page.

1. Click **Database**.

   ![](assets/single-flow-action-from-person-detail-page-1.png)

1. Locate the desired person.

   ![](assets/single-flow-action-from-person-detail-page-2.png)

1. Click the **Person Actions** drop-down and select the flow step of your choice. In this example we'll use [Send Email](/help/marketo/product-docs/core-marketo-concepts/smart-campaigns/flow-actions/send-email.md).

   ![](assets/single-flow-action-from-person-detail-page-3.png)

1. Select the desired email and click **Run Now**.

   ![](assets/single-flow-action-from-person-detail-page-4.png)

>[!NOTE]
>
>If you navigate to a Person Detail page directly (i.e., via link) instead of coming from a page/asset tied to a Workspace, you'll have to choose a Workspace in Step 4.
