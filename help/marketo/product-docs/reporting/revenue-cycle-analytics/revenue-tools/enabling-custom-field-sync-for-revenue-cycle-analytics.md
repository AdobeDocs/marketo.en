# Enabling Custom Field Sync for [!UICONTROL Revenue Cycle Analytics] {#enabling-custom-field-sync-for-revenue-cycle-analytics}

The following steps will allow you to utilize custom fields in RCA reports.

1. Click **[!UICONTROL Admin]**.

   ![](assets/one.png)

1. Click **[!UICONTROL Revenue Cycle Analytics]**, then **[!UICONTROL Custom Field Sync]**.

   ![](assets/two.png)

1. Select your **[!UICONTROL Field Name]**, then click **[!UICONTROL Edit Sync Option]**.

   ![](assets/three.png)

1. Under **[!UICONTROL Sync Status]**, select **[!UICONTROL Enabled]**, then click **[!UICONTROL Save]**.

   ![](assets/four.png)

1. The green check lets you know that the field is set up to sync.

   ![](assets/five.png)

   And that's that!

   >[!NOTE]
   >
   >After the field is enabled, the data will be available in [!UICONTROL Revenue Cycle Analytics] the next day.
