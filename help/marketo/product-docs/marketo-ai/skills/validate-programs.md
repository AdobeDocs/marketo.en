# Validate programs {#validate-programs}

Audit your programs for best practices across all components, such as emails, landing pages, campaigns, and more.

>[!AVAILABILITY]
>
>This feature is currently in open beta. To request access, contact your account manager. You must also agree to the [Core Gen-AI terms and the supplemental terms](https://www.adobe.com/legal/terms/enterprise-licensing/genai-ww.html){target="_blank"}.

## How to use {#how-to-use}

1. In your My Marketo, click the **Marketo AI** tile.

   ![](assets/validate-programs-1.png)

1. Select the **Validate programs** skill.

1. Select the program you want to validate in the right pane.

   ![](assets/validate-programs-3.png){width="800" zoomable="yes"}

   A summary of the selected program appears in the center pane.
   
1. In the prompt window, enter "validate program" and click **Send**.

   The AI Assistant provides a QA of the selected program, showing you what passed and what failed.

   ![](assets/validate-programs-6.png)
