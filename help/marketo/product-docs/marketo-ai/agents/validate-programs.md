# Validate programs {#validate-programs}

Audit your programs for best practices across all components, such as emails, landing pages, campaigns, and more.

## How to use {#how-to-use}

1. In your My Marketo, click the **Build with AI** tile.

   ![](assets/program-qa-1.png)

1. Select the **Validate programs** agent.

1. Select the program you want to validate in the right pane.

   ![](assets/program-qa-3.png){width="800" zoomable="yes"}

   A summary of the selected program appears in the center pane.

   ![](assets/program-qa-4.png){width="450" zoomable="yes"}

1. In the prompt window, enter "validate program" and click **Send**.

   The AI Assistant provides a QA of the selected program, showing you what passed and what failed.

   ![](assets/program-qa-6.png)
