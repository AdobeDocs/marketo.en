# Is Sales Insight Actions Compatible with Salesforce Lightning? {#is-sales-insight-actions-compatible-with-salesforce-lightning}

Yes, Sales Insight Actions is compatible with Salesforce Lightning.

Please [click here](https://s3.amazonaws.com/tout-user-store/salesforce/assets/SF+Guide+for+Lightning.pdf) for the Installation and Success Guide.

>[!NOTE]
>
>Sales Insight Actions buttons for bulk actions are available in the Lead and Contact list views.
