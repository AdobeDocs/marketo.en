## Announcements {#announcements}

* **Account Insight Browser Plug-in Deprecation**: Adobe is removing the Target Account Management [Account Insight browser plug-in](/help/marketo/product-docs/target-account-management/setup-tam/account-insight-plug-in-overview.md){target="_blank"} from the Chrome Web Store on March 18, 2024. Existing users: you can continue to use the plug-in until you migrate your Marketo Engage instance to Adobe Identity and Admin Console.
